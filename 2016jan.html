BETWEEN THE COVERS Book Club is Serious

January 2016

Title:

All the Light We Cannot See

Author:

Anthony Doerr

Discussion Date:

Sunday, January 10th @ 1pm

Discussion Location:

Bistro Campagne

Roll Call:

Patrick, Mike, Alyssa G, Meghan, Bridget, Ellyn

Number of Readers:

4

Book Club Minutes

Out first successful bookclub meeting of the year with a decent turn out to boot! Patrick hosted this month's book and picked a great location to host, the Bistro Campagne in Ravenswood. Even though the Bistro Campagne lost our reservation it was easily forgot with the fantastic food. The crowd favorite was by far the Duck Confit Hash.

Discussion of the book was definitely the best we have had in quite some time. Everyone was a huge fan of Marie and how she escaped mostly unharmed by the tragedies of war. For some it was hard to overcome the pointless deaths of Werner and Fredrick. A main focal point was how we watch as these characters grew up and try to fight their own destiny only to die in the end. There were also heroes and revolutionaries that we came to love including Madem Manec and Etienne LeBlanc. Overall the reception of the book amongst the group was positive with the exception of Ellyn.

After brunch we were able to stroll over to the local Chicago Public Library and do some browsing. We selected next month's book and took a selfie between the shelves!
